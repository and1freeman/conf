**Open:**  
:term(inal)

**Go to vim normal mode(regular vim abilities):**  
C-\ C-n

**Return to terminal:**  
i

**Vim command in terminal:**  
C-w :

**Move focus to the next window:**  
C-w C-w

**Paste register:**  
C-w " {reg}

**Kill job:**  
C-w C-c

**Split window vertically and open terminal:**  
:vert(ical) terminal
